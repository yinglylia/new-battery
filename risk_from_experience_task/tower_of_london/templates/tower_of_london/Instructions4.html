Task navigation:
Move the balls between the rods on the lower panel to match the configuration display above.

  1. Click on the ball you want to move. A white halo will appear and mark the ball as selected.
  2. Then click on the rod where you want the ball to move to. The ball will automatically appear on your selected target location.

You can de-select a ball by clicking on it a second time. However, this will be counted as a move.

Therefore, it is important that you plan your moves before you start the execution.

If you are ready to begin with the first example configuration, press CONTINUE.
The 1-minute timer will start immediately.
