Great, now you know how to move your player and how to open doors.

During the real games, there will be another independent player on the field. This player will also try to reach a treasure. You both pursue independent treasures, and you can both win a treasure in each round. When a player gets stuck behind a door, the other player has the option to open the door using one of their own keys.

Three more rules to be aware of:

  • If a player hasn't picked a path after 10 seconds, they will get assigned to a path and the round starts automatically.
  • When a player has died, they can still use their keys to open doors for the other player.
  • But once a player has reached their treasure, they cannot use their remaining keys anymore to open a door for the other player.

To try how the game works with a second player on the field, you can play four more practice rounds of the game now. Each game takes about a minute.
