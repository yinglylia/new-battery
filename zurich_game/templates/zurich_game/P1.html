The Treasure Game

Goal of the game

You will now play a game in which you have to move a yellow player (YOU) to a treasure using the mouse. A treasure is worth {{ Constants.bonus_for_treasure }}.

You will play multiple rounds of the game and in each round, you will be randomly and anonymously connected to another player. This player will also try to reach a treasure on the same field. You both pursue independent treasures, and you can both win a treasure in each round.

At the end, one of the rounds will be randomly drawn and in case you had reached your treasure in this round, you will receive {{ Constants.bonus_for_treasure }} as a bonus.

How to play

You must first choose one of two paths. Randomly falling red and blue doors on the paths can stop you from moving towards the treasure. A total of six doors fall randomly on the paths chosen by you and the other player, as well as on the paths not chosen. You can open these doors with keys at your disposal. You need red keys for red doors and blue keys for blue doors. In the right part of the display you can see the doors still to come and the keys available to you and the other player.

Some rules to know:

  • You only have 60 seconds to reach the treasure. The time starts running when you choose one of the paths. The remaining time is shown on the game display.
  • Each key can only be used once.
  • If a door has not been opened, the player behind it will die after 7 seconds, losing the possibility of obtaining the treasure.
